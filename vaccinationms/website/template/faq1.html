Coviesafe FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. Is there any registration charge to be paid?

No, there is no registration charge.

2.Is it necessary to register again for 2nd dose or Precaution dose?

No, registration is required only once to create a beneficiary account on Vaccination Management System.
Thereafter, appointments can be booked, both online or onsite, and vaccination can be availed from the same account.
It is recommended that a beneficiary should register only once so that proper records are maintained.
It is also recommended that the registration should be done through an active mobile number to avoid problems in future.

3. Which age groups can register for vaccination on the Vaccination Management portal?

All beneficiaries who have completed 12 years of age and above on the day of registration
(birth year 2010 or earlier) can register for vaccination.

4.How can I find the nearest vaccination center?

You can search in Vaccination Management portal for the vaccination center nearest to your location by PIN code or district,
on the home page of Vaccination Management portal.

5.What information is available regarding the vaccination schedules on Vaccination Management System?

When you search for vaccination centers, the list of centers and the sessions published for each of the vaccination center on various dates,
is displayed on screen. The number of vaccination slots available in the session are also displayed on screen.
